Maple Mustard Salmon

It may seem like you’re going against the stream, but infusing this fish dish with a little sweetness always has our family running into the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 -lb (500 g) salmon filet, bones removed and cut into 4 pieces
  • 1/4 cup (60 mL) Becel® Buttery Taste *
  • 1/4 cup (60 mL) maple syrup
  • 4 tsp. (20 mL) Dijon mustard or grainy Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp. (5 mL) low sodium soy sauce

      Instructions

      1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Arrange salmon in 8-inch (20-cm) baking dish.
      2. Microwave Becel® Buttery Taste margarine at HIGH until melted about 15 seconds. Stir in maple syrup, Dijon mustard and soy sauce. Pour over salmon.
      3. Bake until salmon flakes with a fork, about 12 minutes. Serve, if desired, with hot rice and vegetables.

      Delicious with your favourite rice and vegetable side dishes.

      *Becel® Gold in Quebec.

      To cook on the grill, arrange salmon in center of doubled 18 x 18-in. (46 x 46-cm.) piece heavy-duty aluminum foil, then top with mustard sauce. Wrap foil loosely around salmon, sealing edges airtight with double fold. Arrange packet on grill and cook until salmon flakes with a fork, about 12 minutes.
