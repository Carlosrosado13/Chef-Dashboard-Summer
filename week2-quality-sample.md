First 5 ingredients:
1. 1 cup sushi rice, cooked
2. 2 Tbsp rice vinegar
3. 1 tsp sugar
4. 0.5 lb sashimi-grade tuna, finely chopped
5. 3 Tbsp Japanese mayonnaise (Kewpie)

First 5 steps:
1. Cook sushi rice according to package directions. While warm, mix in the rice vinegar and sugar.
2. Press the seasoned rice firmly into a small rectangular pan or baking dish, about 1/2 inch thick. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour, or until firm.
3. While the rice chills, prepare the tuna. In a medium bowl, combine the finely chopped tuna, Japanese mayonnaise, sriracha, sesame oil, and sliced scallions. Mix gently until just combined. Refrigerate until ready to use.
4. Once the rice is firm, cut it into small rectangles (about 1x2 inches).
5. In a large skillet, heat about an inch of vegetable oil over medium-high heat.

4. 5 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
5. 2 Tbsp melted butter

First 5 steps:
